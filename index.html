What type of food does Gusto Abuja serve? +
We offer a rich selection of dishes including signature meals, grills, continental favourites, and local Nigerian cuisine — crafted for both casual dining and special occasions.
How do I make a reservation? +
Simply call us on 0809 666 6344, send a WhatsApp message, or walk in. We accommodate both reservations and walk-in guests. For large groups or private events, we recommend calling ahead.
Do you host private events or group dining? +
Yes. We host private events, birthday dinners, corporate lunches and group bookings. Contact us via WhatsApp or phone to discuss your event requirements and availability.
What are your opening hours? +
Gusto Abuja opens at 12 pm daily. For exact closing times or holiday hours, call us on 0809 666 6344 or check our Google Maps listing.
Where exactly are you located? +
We are located at 42 Adetokunbo Ademola Cres, Wuse, Abuja 904101 — inside M&G Plaza. Our Plus Code is 3FCP+GH Abuja.
Do you offer takeaway or delivery? +
Please contact us directly on WhatsApp or by phone to ask about our current takeaway and delivery options.
Is parking available? +
Yes, parking is available at M&G Plaza where we are located. There is space for guests arriving by car. For busy periods, street parking nearby is also accessible.
